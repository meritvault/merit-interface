Guide

Deposit

  • Connect Your Wallet
  • Navigate To The Vault You Want To Deposit To.
  • Input The Amount You Want To Deposit
  • Select Stake Button
  • Check The Vault Panel For Your Reward Balance

Withdraw

  • Connect Your Participating Wallet
  • Navigate To Your Staking Vault
  • Select Withdraw Button
  • Input The Amount Or Use The Slider
  • Select Unstaked

When you Withdraw Your Assets, All Rewards Earned Will Be Automatically Claimed
