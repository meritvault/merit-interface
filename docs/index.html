With the rapid developments of the Andromeda network, we aim to support and provide value added services to the Metis community, being in the forefront of developing valuable products to expand the Metis ecosystem.

Ecosystem

Our ecosystem offers the following products to Metis community:

SAAS

Staking as a Service will be the first product by Merit, where users will be able to stake selected projects on Metis network to earn rewards. For every withdrawal from the Merit Vault, a 2% fee will incur, and be added to the Merit treasury. Merit treasury is governed by the mVault DAO.

SAAS NFT
They are a unique NFT received upon depositing to the staking contract, these NFTs are backed by the amount staked in the contract, making the NFT valuable in itself, transferable and tradeable. The NFT will also be used to govern the Merit Vault or mVault.

INDEX

Merit's second product will be the Merit index fund. In the past few years, traditional markets have evolved with the addition of Index funds which has proven to be one of the best ways to make attractive income. focused on creating a lucrative investment in the Metis ecosystem by building a portfolio of best performing assets on Metis network.

DAPP WALLET

To make investment easier for both new and current crypto investors, Merit's final planned product is the fiat-on-ramp integrated non custodial wallet, targeting diverse regions. The Merit wallet will be the central hub for trading and investing in the Merit ecosystem, it will be combining our SAAS and INDEX with other dapp wallet features such as multi-chain bridge, multi-chain dex swap, all in one simple wallet.
